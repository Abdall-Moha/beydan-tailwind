Our story

A luxury Somali dining room built for taste, comfort, and celebration.

Beydaan Restaurant brings refined hospitality to Digfeer Road with elegant plates, warm service, and flavors inspired by Mogadishu's coastal energy.

Beydaan experience

Modern dining with Somali soul.

Our kitchen is built around fragrant rice, premium grilled meats, seafood, handmade sauces, and desserts served with care. Every table is designed to feel calm, polished, and welcoming.

Guests visit for family dinners, business meals, birthdays, and relaxed nights where the food feels special from first plate to tea.
